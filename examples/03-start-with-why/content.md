- No funding
- Bicycle shop mechanics
- Team paid with own money

## Slide 11: TiVo Failure

**Why TiVo Failed**

TiVo: First-mover, best product, well-funded

Their pitch (WHAT-focused):
> "We have a product that pauses live TV, skips commercials, rewinds, and memorizes your viewing habits."

Consumer response: "I don't believe you."

**TiVo should have said**:
> "If you're the kind of person who likes total control over every aspect of your life, we have a product for you."

**Result**: TiVo is a commercial failure despite superior product.

## Slide 14: Summary

**Key Takeaways**

1. **People don't buy WHAT you do, they buy WHY you do it.**

2. **The goal is not to sell to people who need what you have. The goal is to sell to people who believe what you believe.**

3. **If you hire people just because they can do a job, they'll work for your money. But if you hire people who believe what you believe, they'll work with blood, sweat, and tears.**

4. **What you do simply proves what you believe.**

5. **The goal is not to be better than the competition. The goal is to be better than yourself.**
