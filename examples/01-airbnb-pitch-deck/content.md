# Airbnb Pitch Deck (2009)

Early pitch deck used by AirBed&Breakfast to raise funding.

---

## Slide 1: Title

**AirBed&Breakfast**

Book rooms with locals, rather than hotels.

> Layout: Centered title with tagline

---

## Slide 2: Problem

**Problem**

- Price is an important concern for customers booking travel online.
- Hotels leave you disconnected from the city and its culture.
- No easy way exists to book a room with a local or become a host.

> Layout: Title + 3 bullet points, vertical list

---

## Slide 3: Solution

**Solution**

A web platform where users can rent out their space to host travelers to:

- **SAVE MONEY** - when traveling
- **MAKE MONEY** - when hosting
- **SHARE CULTURE** - local connection to the city

> Layout: Title + intro text + 3 value proposition boxes stacked vertically

---

## Slide 4: Market Validation

**Market Validation**

- **630,000** on temporary housing site couchsurfing.com
- **17,000** temporary housing listings on SF & NYC Craigslist from 07/09 – 07/16

> Layout: Two large numbers with descriptions

---

## Slide 5: Market Size

**Market Size**

- **1.9 Billion+** TRIPS BOOKED (WORLDWIDE) - Total Available Market
- **532M** BUDGET & ONLINE TRIPS - Serviceable Available Market
- **10.6M** TRIPS W/AB&B - Market Share

> Layout: 3 circles of decreasing size showing TAM → SAM → Market Share

---

## Slide 6: Product

**Product**

Flow: SEARCH BY CITY → REVIEW LISTINGS → BOOK IT!

> Layout: 3 product screenshots showing the user flow with arrows connecting them

---

## Slide 7: Business Model

**Business Model**

We take a 10% commission on each transaction.

- **10.6M** TRIPS W/AB&B (Share of Market)
- **$20** AVG FEE ($70/night @ 3 nights)
- **$200M** REVENUE (2008-2011)

> Layout: Title + description + funnel diagram with 3 metrics flowing down

---

## Slide 8: Market Adoption

**Market Adoption**

Three channels:

1. **EVENTS** - target events monthly
   - Octoberfest (6M), Cebit (700,000), Summerfest (1M), Eurocup (3M+), Mardi Gras (800,000)
   - with listing widget

2. **PARTNERSHIPS** - cheap/alternative travel
   - Partner logos: GoLoco, Kayak, Orbitz

3. **CRAIGSLIST** - dual posting feature
   - AirBnB ↔ Craigslist integration

> Layout: 3-column layout, each with title + description + visual

---

## Slide 9: Competition

**Competition**

2x2 matrix:
- X-axis: OFFLINE TRANSACTION ← → ONLINE TRANSACTION
- Y-axis: AFFORDABLE ↑ / EXPENSIVE ↓

Competitors positioned:
- Top-left (Affordable/Offline): CouchSurfing, Craigslist, BedandBreakfast.com
- Top-right (Affordable/Online): **AirBed&Breakfast** (our position)
- Bottom-left (Expensive/Offline): VRBO, rentdot.com
- Bottom-right (Expensive/Online): hotels.com, Hostels.com

> Layout: 2x2 competitive positioning matrix with logos

---

## Slide 10: Competitive Advantages

**Competitive Advantages**

6 advantages in 2x3 grid:

| 1st TO MARKET | HOST INCENTIVE |
| EASE OF USE | PROFILES |
| LIST ONCE | DESIGN & BRAND |

> Layout: 2x3 grid of advantage cards, each with title and description

---

## Notes

- Original deck from ~2009, used to raise seed funding
- 10 core slides (excluding promotional slides at end)
- Visual style: Clean, blue accent color, large typography for key numbers
- Key elements: Market sizing funnel, competitive 2x2 matrix, product screenshots
