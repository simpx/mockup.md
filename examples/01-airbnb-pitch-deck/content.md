# Airbnb Pitch Deck (2009)

经典创业融资演示，Airbnb 早期用于种子轮融资的 pitch deck。这份 deck 帮助他们获得了 Y Combinator 的支持，是创业界最具标志性的融资演示之一。

---

## Slide 1: Title

**AirBed & Breakfast**

Book rooms with locals, rather than hotels.

---

## Slide 2: Problem

**Problem**

- Price is an important concern for customers booking travel online
- Hotels leave you disconnected from the city and its culture
- No easy way exists to book a room with a local or become a host

**Key Insight**: 旅行者想要更便宜、更有当地特色的住宿体验，但市场上没有好的解决方案。

---

## Slide 3: Solution

**Solution**

A web platform where users can:
- **Travelers**: Book space from verified locals
- **Hosts**: Rent out extra space to travelers

Benefits:
- Save money when traveling
- Make money when hosting
- Share local culture

---

## Slide 4: Market Validation

**Market Validation**

- 630,000+ temporary housing listings on Craigslist (SF, NYC, Paris)
- Couchsurfing.org has 630,000+ members
- Budget travel is a $59 billion industry

**Proof**: 市场需求真实存在，但现有解决方案体验差。

---

## Slide 5: Market Size

**Market Size**

| Segment | Size |
|---------|------|
| Total Available Market | $1.9 Billion |
| - Trip Planning | $10.6 B |
| - Budget/Hostels | $15 B |
| - Online Vacation Rentals | $256 M |

**Bottom-up calculation**:
- 560M total trips booked online
- 10% budget travelers = 56M
- $49 avg booking = $2.7B market

---

## Slide 6: Product

**Product**

How it works:
1. Browse listings by city
2. Contact host
3. Book and pay online
4. Travel and stay
5. Leave a review

Features:
- Verified profiles
- Secure payments
- Reviews & ratings
- Messaging system
- Photo galleries

---

## Slide 7: Business Model

**Business Model**

**Revenue**: 10% commission on each booking
- 3% from guest
- 7% from host

**Example**:
- $80/night booking
- Airbnb earns $8
- 2 night average stay = $16 per transaction

---

## Slide 8: Adoption Strategy

**Go-to-Market Strategy**

Dual approach:
1. **Events**: Target major conferences and events where hotels are sold out
   - SXSW, Inaugurations, World Cup, Olympics
2. **Organic**: SEO + Word of mouth from satisfied users

Traction channels:
- Craigslist cross-posting
- Social media referrals
- PR coverage
